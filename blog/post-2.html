2026.06 · 可穿戴系统Wearable Systems

可穿戴系统入门:从柔性传感器到数据可视化Wearable Systems 101: From Flexible Sensors to Data Viz

想做一件「会感知」的衣服,最该先想清楚的是数据链路,而不是先把传感器焊上去。一条最小可行链路只有三步:采集、传输、可视化。 To make a "perceiving" garment, clarify the data chain before soldering any sensor. A minimal viable chain has just three steps: sense, transmit, visualize.

1. 采集1. Sense

柔性传感器(压阻布、应变纱线、织物电极)输出的是微弱模拟信号,通常需要一个带放大与滤波的前端,再用 MCU 的 ADC 采样。 Flexible sensors (piezoresistive fabric, strain yarn, textile electrodes) output faint analog signals — usually needing an amplified, filtered frontend before MCU ADC sampling.

2. 传输2. Transmit

低功耗场景首选 BLE;数据量大再考虑 Wi-Fi。注意给每帧加上时间戳与传感器编号,后期对齐才不头疼。 BLE is the default for low power; choose Wi-Fi when throughput demands it. Stamp each frame with a timestamp and sensor id to ease later alignment.

3. 可视化3. Visualize

接到网页端用 Canvas 或轻量图表库实时画线,先把「看得见」跑通,再谈算法。常见的坑:电源噪声、接触不良、采样率与动画帧率不一致。 Pipe it to the browser and draw live lines with Canvas or a light chart lib — get "visible" working before any algorithm. Common pitfalls: power noise, poor contact, and sample/animation rate mismatch.

跑通这条链路,你就拥有了一个可穿戴系统的「hello world」,后面所有研究都建立在它之上。 Once this chain works, you have a wearable "hello world" — every later study builds on it.
