关于我About

我是花健,苏州大学服装设计与工程方向博士研究生,导师为薛哲彬教授。 我拥有交叉学科背景:本科毕业于电子信息工程,硕士攻读材料与化工——"硬件 + 材料"的训练让我天然地把服装当作可计算、可传感的系统来思考。 我的研究聚焦 数字时尚与可穿戴系统: 一方面用 3D 建模、虚拟试穿与生成式设计拓展服装的数字表达; 另一方面探索柔性传感与智能织物,让服装成为可感知、可交互的载体。 I am Jian Hua, a Ph.D. student in Fashion Design & Engineering at Soochow University, advised by Prof. Xue Zhebin. My background is cross-disciplinary: a B.Eng. in Electronic Information Engineering and an M.Eng. in Materials and Chemical Engineering. That "hardware + materials" training leads me to treat garments as computable, sensable systems. My research centers on Digital Fashion & Wearable Systems: on one hand, using 3D modeling, virtual try-on, and generative design to expand the digital expression of garments; on the other, exploring flexible sensing and smart textiles to make clothing a perceivable, interactive medium.

我持续在人机交互、计算机图形学与电子织物方向深入,也在整理本科与硕士阶段的作品与项目。 欢迎通过下方方式与我交流或探讨合作。 I keep deepening my background in HCI, computer graphics, and e-textiles, and organizing my undergraduate and master's works and projects. Feel free to reach out for discussion or collaboration.

研究方向Research

01

数字时尚Digital Fashion

3D 服装建模、虚拟试穿、生成式设计与数字资产管线。 3D garment modeling, virtual try-on, generative design, and digital asset pipelines.

02

可穿戴系统Wearable Systems

柔性传感、智能织物与人体生理/动作信号采集。 Flexible sensing, smart textiles, and human physiological / motion signal acquisition.

03

柔性电子Flexible Electronics

结合电子与材料背景,研发可穿戴的柔性电路与器件。 Bridging electronics and materials to build flexible circuits and devices for wearables.

04

人体与动作Body & Motion

动作捕捉、虚拟人驱动与服装动画仿真。 Motion capture, virtual-human driving, and garment animation simulation.

05

可持续与设计计算Sustainable & Design Computing

参数化设计、可持续制造与数据驱动的设计方法。 Parametric design, sustainable manufacturing, and data-driven design methods.
