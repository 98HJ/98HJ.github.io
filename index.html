关于我About

我是华建,将于 2026 年秋季进入苏州大学服装设计与工程专业攻读博士学位。 我的研究聚焦 Digital Fashion & Wearable Systems(数字时尚与可穿戴系统): 一方面用 3D 建模、虚拟试穿与生成式设计拓展服装的数字表达; 另一方面探索柔性传感与智能织物,让服装成为可感知、可交互的载体。 I am Hua Jian, an incoming Ph.D. student in Fashion Design & Engineering at Soochow University (Fall 2026). My research centers on Digital Fashion & Wearable Systems: on one hand, using 3D modeling, virtual try-on, and generative design to expand the digital expression of garments; on the other, exploring flexible sensing and smart textiles to make clothing a perceivable, interactive medium.

在正式入学前,我在补强人机交互、计算机图形学与电子织物方面的功底,也在整理本科阶段的作品与项目。 欢迎通过下方方式与我交流或探讨合作。 Before formally starting, I am strengthening my background in HCI, computer graphics, and e-textiles, and organizing my undergraduate works and projects. Feel free to reach out for discussion or collaboration.

可穿戴系统Wearable Systems
